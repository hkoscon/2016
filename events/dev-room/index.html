Dev room is a open space for open source developers/contributors to code, discuss and contribute on open source projects.

Dev room is located at Training Room 1 at Core F (behind Lecture Theatre), open on from 09:30 on Day 1 and Day 2 to 16:00 (Day 1) and 17:00 (Day 2).

We suggests that developers/contributors can fill up a online form, so everyone can check out on a public online spreadsheet and find any interesting topics. And don't forget to tweet it with hashtag #hkoscon !

Dev room are open on Day 1 and 2.

If you're coming, please fill in this form to let everyone know:
