Overview

Hong Kong Open Source Conference 2016 is an annual open source conference organised by the community, to connect global open source technology.

We would like to invite developers and contributors from Hong Kong and overseas to submit proposals.

Most of sessions/activities accepted through CFP will be presented/delivered on 2016/6/25 Saturday, in seminar room setting with audience size of 50-60 for general talks (except keynotes). Workshops, hackathons and sessions with longer length may be moved to 6/26 Sunday (in classroom setting with audience size of 30-70) if the conference is extended. Zero to Few talks might be move to Friday (in seminar room setting with audience size of 200).

HKOSCon provides financial aid program to selected overseas speakers/contributors who speak at HKOSCon 2016. (financial aid application is also part of the submission form)

Topics

Topics of interest include but are not limited to:

  • Internet of Things
  • Data
  • Cloud
  • DevOps

Important Dates

  • Call For Proposals (CFP) Deadline: 2016/02/28 Sunday 23:00 HKT (GMT+8)
  • Announcement of CFP Result: by 2016/03/31

HKOSCon 2016 information

Date: 2016/06/24-26 (Friday - Sunday)

Website: http://2016.opensource.hk
