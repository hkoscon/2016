QiLiu is the co-founder of PingCAP. He has over 10+ years of experience in system infrastructure and software technologies. He is also the co-author of open source project TiDB and Codis. Prior to PingCAP, he joined Wandoulabs in Distributed System Team. Prior to Wandoulabs, he was in the infrastructure team of JingDong.
