Sponsor Prospectus

To cover expenses of conference, we are calling for sponsorships of HKOSCon 2016 and look for potential sponsors.

If you interested to sponsor HKOSCon 2016, please contact with us.

Contact HKOSCon »

In becoming a sponsor, we are offering to you:

  • Matchmaking opportunities for companies with local enterprises;
  • Marketing opportunities to be featured in conference materials and presented in media announcements;
  • Networking with highly capable IT professionals

The conference has proven to attract participants usually scattered in their own different groups. By becoming a sponsor, your organisation will be able to build relationships with them. Please refer to our event web site at http://2016.opensource.hk/ .

Enquiry and Application

Please contact us at conference@opensource.hk to obtain Call For Sponsorship document and further information.
